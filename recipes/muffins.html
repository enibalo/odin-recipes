Muffins

Description

Chocolate chip muffins are my favoirte dessert to make. I hope you enjoy them as much as I do.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Egg
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. Whisk flour, baking powder and salt together.
  3. Whisk butter until it is soft and pale yellow.
  4. Add in a steady-stream of sugar to the butter mixture until it becomes light and fluffy.
  5. Add in eggs to butter mixture until just-combined.
  6. Add in a 1/4 of flour mixture, then 1/3 of milk to butter mixture. Repating this order of mixing until all ingredients have been added together. Do not overmix the batter.
  7. Add in the chocolate chips.
  8. Put the muffins in a cupcake tray and leave it in the oven for 29 minutes.
